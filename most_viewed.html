The Internet™ Webpages Example.com

The second-level domain example.com is reserved by the Internet Assigned Numbers Authority along with example.net, example.org, and example.edu. The websites are operated by the Internet Corporation for Assigned Names and Numbers and commonly used in education, tutorials, and as placeholder domains in other areas.

History

The history of these example domain names is not very interesting. The earliest known usage of them is from 1992.1

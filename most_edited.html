Taco Bell is hard to hate.

Taco Bell is a pretty good fast food restaurant that was founded by Glen Bell in 1962. Bell got into the restaurant business through his hot dog stand, Bell's Drive-In. His idea for Taco Bell came after witnessing the popularity of a nearby Mexican restaurant. Taco Bell quickly became a popular franchise, opening 325 restaurants in its first 8 years.1

In 2015, the building of the first Taco Bell was scheduled for demolition. However, the good people at Taco Bell wouldn't let that happen and quickly rescued the building, transporting it to Taco Bell's corporate office, where it currently sits in the parking lot.
